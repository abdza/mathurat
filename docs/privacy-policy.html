Privacy Policy

App: Al-Mathurat
Developer: Abdullah Solutions
Last updated: 22 March 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Al-Mathurat Android application handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Information We Collect

Al-Mathurat does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and does not communicate with any external server.

Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following data locally on your device only, using Android's SharedPreferences:

  • Recitation counts — how many times you have tapped each zikr item per session and version.
  • Completion history — a record of which sessions you have completed on which dates, used to display the calendar view and achievements.
  • Zikir Counter value — the current count in the standalone Zikir Counter.
  • Settings — your preferred language, Arabic font size, and transliteration toggle.

All of this data remains on your device at all times. It is never uploaded, shared, or accessible to anyone other than you.

Internet Access

Al-Mathurat does not require or use an internet connection. No network requests are made by the app.

Third-Party Services

Al-Mathurat does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics tools, advertising networks, or crash reporting services.

Children's Privacy

Al-Mathurat is suitable for all ages. Since no personal data is collected, there are no special considerations regarding children's privacy.
